Curriculum Alignment

Victorian Curriculum F–10 Version 2.0

All resources are fully aligned to the Victorian Curriculum Version 2.0 HPE strand. Mandatory for all Victorian government and Catholic schools from 2026. Swimming and water safety is a mandated priority area in the Safety (S) and Lifelong Physical Activities (LPA) focus areas.
